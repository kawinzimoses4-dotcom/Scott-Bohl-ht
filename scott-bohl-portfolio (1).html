How the numbers run

Scott Bohl builds the financial backbone behind organizations that have to keep running no matter what: power grids, restaurants, and water systems. As Financial Manager at Orlando Utilities Commission, he turns capital plans and operating budgets into the financial discipline a public utility depends on.

His path runs through three different operating environments — power generation, multi-brand hospitality, and municipal utilities — giving him a finance toolkit shaped by real operations, not just spreadsheets.
